Instructions

The focus of this game is to help you strike a balance b/w the accuracy and speed of your typing

You will be scored based on how fast you type the words displayed on screen

On successfully typing more than 5 words correctly, your score multiplies. But beware, one wrong key and your multiplier gets reset

It's a race against time. The words are thrown at you at an increasing pace. The game ends if there are 7 untyped words on the screen.

WORD RACE

A typing game implemented using the MERN stack
